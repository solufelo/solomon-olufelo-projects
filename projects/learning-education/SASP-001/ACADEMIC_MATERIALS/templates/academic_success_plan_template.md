# Academic Success Plan Template

## Instructions for Use
This template provides the structure and guidance for creating an authentic, heartfelt Academic Success Plan that maintains your unique voice while meeting all academic requirements. Each section includes prompts and examples to help generate genuine, personal content.

**Before You Begin**:
1. Review your Personal Context documents for authentic experiences to reference
2. Consult the AI Training System guidelines for voice consistency
3. Check the Memory System for relevant themes and patterns to integrate
4. Keep the course rubric nearby for requirement verification

---

## Chart 1: Obstacle Identification

**Requirement**: Identify minimum 5 obstacles (mix of academic and personal) that have impacted your academic success.

### Template Structure:

| Category | Obstacle | Specific Example | Impact on Academics |
|----------|----------|------------------|---------------------|
| Academic | [Specific academic challenge] | [Personal experience when this happened] | [How it affected your performance] |
| Academic | [Another academic challenge] | [Another specific example] | [Impact description] |
| Personal | [Personal obstacle] | [When/how you experienced this] | [Academic consequences] |
| Personal | [Another personal challenge] | [Specific situation/example] | [Effect on studies] |
| [Academic/Personal] | [Fifth obstacle] | [Personal example] | [Academic impact] |

### Voice Guidelines for This Section:
- **Tone**: Honest and reflective, acknowledging real struggles without self-pity
- **Examples**: Use specific situations, times, and feelings
- **Language**: "I struggled with..." "I found myself..." "When I tried to..."
- **Emotion**: Balanced vulnerability - show the difficulty but also your awareness

### Sample Authentic Phrasing:
- "Managing textbook readings became overwhelming when I realized I was falling behind in multiple courses..."
- "Anxiety would hit me especially hard before major assignments, making it difficult to even start..."
- "I noticed I was constantly underestimating how long assignments would take..."

- [ ] Content sounds like your genuine voice and experience

- [ ] Growth mindset and learning orientation are evident

### Academic Requirements
- [ ] All chart requirements are fully met
- [ ] SMART goal includes all five components clearly
- [ ] Summary synthesizes rather than repeats previous content
- [ ] Action plan covers all required assessments

### Consistency and Flow
- [ ] References between sections are natural and smooth
- [ ] Themes develop logically throughout the document
- [ ] Voice tone is appropriate for each section type
- [ ] Overall narrative is coherent and meaningful

### Technical Quality
- [ ] Grammar, spelling, and punctuation are correct
- [ ] Document is well-organized and easy to follow
- [ ] All sections are complete and substantial
- [ ] Word count and format requirements are met
