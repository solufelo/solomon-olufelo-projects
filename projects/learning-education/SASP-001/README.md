## ğŸ“‹ Academic Context

### Course: Student Academic Success (SAS) Program
- **Institution**: Wilfrid Laurier University
- **Focus**: Self-awareness, obstacle identification, success planning
- **Key Concepts**: SMART goals, reflection, solution generation

### Assignment: Academic Success Plan (8 Components)
1. **Obstacle Identification** (5+ challenges)
2. **Solution Generation** (3 obstacles, 2 solutions each)
3. **Solution Analysis** (3 solutions with detailed breakdown)
4. **SMART Goal Development** (tied to most important solution)
5. **Barrier Prediction** (2 barriers with prevention strategies)
6. **Celebration Planning** (short-term goals and rewards)
7. **Summary** (comprehensive overview and SAS connection)
8. **Action Plan** (detailed strategies for all assessments)
