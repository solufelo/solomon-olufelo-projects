1. **Always read the job posting carefully** - Look for specific keywords and requirements
2. **Choose the closest matching resume** from the categories above
3. **Customize the Professional Summary** to match the specific role
4. **Adjust bullet points** to emphasize most relevant experience
5. **Mirror job posting language** to pass ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)

- All resumes use simple formatting for ATS compatibility
- Keywords are strategically placed throughout

- Avoid tables, images, or complex formatting in final submissions

