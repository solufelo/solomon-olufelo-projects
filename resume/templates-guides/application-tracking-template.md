### Week 1: Initial Application

- [ ] Add to tracking spreadsheet

### Week 2: First Follow-up
- [ ] Send follow-up email if no response
- [ ] Check application status online
- [ ] Engage with company content on social media
- [ ] Update tracking spreadsheet

### Week 3: LinkedIn Connection
- [ ] Connect with hiring manager or team members
- [ ] Send personalized connection message
- [ ] Share relevant content or insights

### Week 4: Final Follow-up
- [ ] Send final follow-up email

## Company Research Checklist

### Before Applying
- [ ] Company mission, values, and culture
- [ ] Recent news and growth trajectory
- [ ] Tech stack and development practices
- [ ] Team size and structure
- [ ] Remote work policies
- [ ] Salary range research (Glassdoor, etc.)
- [ ] Employee reviews and feedback
- [ ] LinkedIn employee connections
- [ ] University alumni connections

### Before Interview
- [ ] Deep dive into company products/services
- [ ] Recent product launches or updates
- [ ] Technical challenges they might face
- [ ] Company culture and values alignment
- [ ] Growth opportunities and career paths
- [ ] Interview process and timeline
- [ ] Preparation for role-specific questions

## Interview Preparation Checklist

### Technical Preparation
- [ ] Review job description requirements
- [ ] Practice relevant coding challenges
- [ ] Prepare project demonstrations
- [ ] Review system design basics
- [ ] Practice explaining technical decisions
- [ ] Prepare questions about tech stack
- [ ] Review company's technical blog/posts

### Behavioral Preparation
- [ ] Prepare STAR method examples
- [ ] Practice common behavioral questions
- [ ] Prepare questions about company culture
- [ ] Review academic context responses
- [ ] Practice elevator pitch
- [ ] Prepare questions about role and growth
- [ ] Review availability and scheduling

### Logistics
- [ ] Confirm interview time and format
- [ ] Test technology (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
- [ ] Prepare quiet, professional space
- [ ] Have resume and portfolio ready
- [ ] Prepare questions for interviewer
- [ ] Plan follow-up thank you email
- [ ] Update tracking spreadsheet

## Usage Instructions

1. **Create a Google Sheet** or Excel file with these templates
2. **Update daily** with new applications and follow-ups
3. **Review weekly** to assess progress and adjust strategy
4. **Use checklists** to ensure thorough preparation
5. **Track metrics** to measure success and identify improvement areas

*Remember: Consistency and organization are key to a successful job search. This tracking system will help you stay on top of applications, follow-ups, and networking opportunities.*
