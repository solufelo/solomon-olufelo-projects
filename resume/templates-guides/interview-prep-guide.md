- Wasp framework for full-stack integration

- Spaced repetition algorithm implementation

**Response Framework:**
1. **Technical Alignment:** Role matches your full-stack development expertise
2. **Growth Opportunity:** Company culture and technology stack align with career goals
3. **Impact Potential:** Opportunity to contribute to meaningful projects

**Response Framework:**
1. **Planning:** Break down tasks and create realistic timelines

**Response Framework:**
1. **Problem Definition:** Clear explanation of the technical challenge
2. **Research & Analysis:** How you investigated and understood the issue

4. **Results & Learning:** Outcome and lessons learned for future projects
