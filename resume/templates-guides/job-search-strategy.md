## Application Strategy

### High-Volume Approach (Phase 1)
- **Target:** 5-10 applications per week
- **Focus:** Quantity to build momentum and interview experience
- **Platforms:** Indeed, LinkedIn, job boards
- **Documentation:** Track all applications in spreadsheet

### Targeted Approach (Phase 2)
- **Target:** 2-3 high-quality applications per week
- **Focus:** Companies that align with goals and values
- **Research:** Deep dive into company culture, tech stack, mission
- **Customization:** Tailored resumes and cover letters

### Referral Strategy (Phase 3)
- **Target:** 1-2 referral applications per week
- **Focus:** Companies where you have connections
- **Approach:** Reach out to contacts before applying
- **Follow-up:** Maintain relationships with all contacts

## Interview Preparation

### Technical Interview Prep
1. **Coding Challenges**
   - LeetCode (JavaScript, Python)
   - HackerRank practice
   - Project-based problem solving
   - Algorithm and data structure review

2. **System Design Basics**
   - Database design principles
   - API architecture
   - Authentication and security
   - Scalability considerations

3. **Project Presentations**
   - CAPTAIN-FUNDS demo
   - StudyOS walkthrough
   - Technical decision explanations
   - Problem-solving methodologies

### Behavioral Interview Prep
1. **STAR Method Examples**
   - Leadership (CLSO Media team management)
   - Problem-solving (AI voice generation innovation)
   - Adaptability (Academic redemption journey)
   - Results (Revenue generation and project delivery)

2. **Common Questions**
   - Why are you leaving your current role?
   - How do you handle tight deadlines?
   - Describe a challenging technical problem you solved
   - How do you stay current with technology?

### Academic Context Handling
1. **Positive Framing**
   - "Currently pursuing BSc Computer Science with expected graduation Spring 2026"
   - "Balancing academic commitments with professional development"
   - "Available for part-time and contract opportunities"

2. **Growth Story**
   - Academic challenges → SASP program → strong performance
   - Demonstrates resilience and determination
   - Shows commitment to continuous improvement

## Company Research Framework

### Before Applying
1. **Company Overview**
   - Mission, values, culture
   - Recent news and growth
   - Tech stack and development practices
   - Team size and structure

2. **Role Analysis**
   - Required vs. preferred qualifications
   - Growth opportunities
   - Remote work flexibility
   - Salary range research

3. **Connection Opportunities**
   - LinkedIn employee connections
   - University alumni
   - Industry contacts
   - Previous collaborators

### Interview Preparation
1. **Company-Specific Research**
   - Recent product launches
   - Technical challenges they face
   - Company culture and values
   - Growth trajectory and opportunities

2. **Role-Specific Preparation**
   - Technical requirements deep dive
   - Team structure and collaboration
   - Development processes and tools
   - Success metrics and expectations

## Risk Mitigation

### Academic Balance
- **Schedule Management:** Block application time around classes
- **Priority Setting:** Academic success remains primary
- **Communication:** Clear expectations about availability
- **Flexibility:** Part-time and contract opportunities prioritized

### Application Quality
- **Customization:** Each application tailored to role
- **Proofreading:** Multiple reviews before submission
- **Documentation:** Consistent professional materials
- **Follow-up:** Systematic approach to all applications
